01 — Package Overview

The Karnali Soul Experience

Surkhet Valley, the gateway to Karnali — Nepal's most unspoiled province — is a destination that rewards the curious traveler with sacred heritage, pristine nature, and authentic community life, all within a single unforgettable day.

This carefully curated package blends spiritual discovery at Deutibajai Temple, serene lakeside moments at Bulbule, ancient ruins at Kakrebihar, and a genuine farm-to-table organic feast that connects you to the land itself.

We don't just show you places. We immerse you in stories, flavors, and encounters that become the memories you carry home.

03 — Tourist Attractions

What Awaits You

01
Spiritual · Heritage

Deutibajai Temple

Perched atop a hill commanding sweeping views of the valley, this revered temple is Surkhet's most iconic landmark. Its spiritual energy and architectural beauty make it an unforgettable opening to the day — particularly magical in morning light.

02
Nature · Serenity

Bulbule Lake

A jewel of natural beauty nestled near the heart of Surkhet, Bulbule Lake offers tranquil boating, birdwatching opportunities, and serene reflection moments surrounded by lush greenery. Perfect for nature lovers and photographers alike.

03
Archaeology · History

Kakrebihar Ruins

Ancient Buddhist monastery ruins dating back centuries, Kakrebihar is a haunting and beautiful site that connects travelers to the deep historical roots of the Karnali region. Walking these grounds is to walk through time itself.

04
Culture · Cuisine

Organic Farm Experience

A hands-on encounter with local farming communities where travelers share a farm-to-table organic lunch, learn traditional food preparation, and engage with the warm hospitality that defines Karnali culture. An experience money rarely buys.

04 — Time Schedule

Your Day, Beautifully Planned

7:00 Morning

🍵Welcome Breakfast & Tour Briefing

Meet your guide at the hotel over a traditional Karnali breakfast — local daal-bhat, fresh fruits, and butter tea. Your guide will walk you through the day's journey, hand out orientation maps, and answer any questions before departure.

8:00 Morning

🛕Deutibajai Temple — Sacred Morning

Begin with the hilltop Deutibajai Temple for panoramic sunrise views. Your guide will narrate the temple's legend, significance, and architectural features. Photography is encouraged. Light incense, observe rituals, and soak in the spiritual atmosphere.

10:30 Morning

🏛️Kakrebihar Archaeological Site

Explore the ancient Buddhist monastery ruins and let history come alive through expert guided narration. Allocated free exploration time allows personal discovery among stone carvings and centuries-old architecture in a serene natural setting.

1:00 Afternoon

🥘Local Organic Lunch at the Farm

Arrive at a partner organic farm for a genuine farm-to-table experience. Enjoy locally grown seasonal vegetables, freshly milled rice, and traditional Karnali dishes. Meet the farming family and optionally help with a brief hands-on activity in the garden.

3:00 Afternoon

🚣Bulbule Lake & Cultural Interaction

A serene visit to Bulbule Lake for optional paddleboat rides and lakeside walks. Followed by a brief community cultural exchange — local women's groups demonstrate traditional crafts, folk songs, and offer handmade souvenirs for purchase.

6:00 Evening

🌅Farewell & Departure

Return to the hotel or departure point as the valley glows in golden hour light. Your guide presents a certificate of visit and a small Karnali souvenir — a handcrafted memento of the day. Feedback forms and warm farewells conclude the journey.
